Note: This Business transaction monitoring tutorial requires WebSphere MQ and a database (DB2Â® or Oracle).

A business transaction is a self-contained business function; for example, the booking of an airline ticket.

On IBM Integration Bus, a business transaction might be implemented by using multiple message flows in different applications.

Business transaction monitoring involves tracking the lifecycle of a message across the message flows that make up a business transaction.
